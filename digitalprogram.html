Announcement

Do you have a startup or business that’s looking to grow through digital marketing, a career in digital marketing, or just want to add digital marketing as one of your skills?

During the pandemic, the urgency to have digital marketing skills have increased greatly following a shift in marketing methods traditionally done offline to online activities. Unfortunately, it still reigns true to a lot of people that digital marketing is merely putting up ads online or SKUs in e-commerce. There is, in fact, a whole world of marketing processes through the digital platform that can be learnt to create measurable and cost-effective techniques that will bring your campaign optimum results.

For you seeking to learn the fundamental guide and practices to master in digital marketing, IF Academy along with dentsuX has just the right event for you! dentsuX is a long time player in the field of communication and media planning and has clients from prominent companies across Indonesia.

You can choose one of our workshops according to your needs, or follow 4 workshops at once with a special price!
